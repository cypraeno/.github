### Welcome to **Cypraeno**!

cypraeno is the home for the Caitlyn MCRT Renderer, a ray-tracer written in C++ developed by our RenderTeam. This organization aims to continue growing in members and projects, and we welcome anyone interested in any projects we may be doing to reach out!
If you want to look into developing for [Caitlyn](https://github.com/cypraeno/caitlyn), feel free to contact the email above or either ctloi@uwaterloo.ca or sbai@uwaterloo.ca.
